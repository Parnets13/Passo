## üéØ Design Principles

### Spacing
- **Generous Padding**: py-20 for sections
- **Consistent Gaps**: gap-6, gap-8 throughout
- **Breathing Room**: No cramped elements
- **Container Max-Width**: 6xl for optimal reading

### Typography
- **Font Hierarchy**:
  - Hero: 5xl-6xl (48px-60px)
  - Section Titles: 4xl (36px)
  - Body: lg-xl (18px-20px)
  - Small: sm (14px)
- **Font Weights**: Bold for headings, semibold for buttons
- **Line Heights**: Generous for readability

### Colors
- **Primary**: #2596be (Brand Blue)
- **Dark**: #1a7a9e (Deep Blue)
- **Light**: #3ab5e0 (Sky Blue)
- **Neutrals**: Gray scale (50-900)
- **Accents**: Category-specific colors

### Shadows
- **Subtle**: shadow-sm for cards
- **Medium**: shadow-lg for elevated elements
- **Strong**: shadow-2xl for modals
- **Colored**: shadow-[#2596be]/30 for brand elements

### Borders
- **Thin**: border-2 for cards
- **Colors**: gray-100, gray-200 for subtle separation
- **Hover**: border-[#2596be] for interaction
- **Radius**: rounded-xl, rounded-2xl for modern look

## üöÄ Interactions

### Hover States
- **Cards**: -translate-y-1 (lift effect)
- **Buttons**: -translate-y-0.5 + shadow increase
- **Icons**: scale-110 (grow effect)
- **Colors**: Transition to brand blue

### Transitions
- **Duration**: 300ms for most interactions
- **Easing**: Default ease for smooth feel
- **Properties**: transform, shadow, colors

### Focus States
- **Inputs**: ring-4 with brand color
- **Buttons**: Outline with brand color
- **Links**: Underline on hover
